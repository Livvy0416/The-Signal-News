Your Signal is your community news: We’re also more than just news

By Joshua Lister · March 26, 2025

If you’re reading this, you already know we have a new website. The team is working hard to build a community resource you can count on and be part of.

We’re launching and revamping initiatives—from restarting our podcast to boosting social media coverage—all to tell your community stories. Our goal is simple: provide news and entertainment that matter to you.

To put it plainly, we are your community media outlet, and our intention is to serve you, our community.

New features include Community Corner under Life & Culture, where you can submit poetry, photography, and more. We’re also proud to expand Nuestra Voz, featuring authentic stories by and for our Hispanic community.

We’re engaging beyond campus: from local businesses to parks, we want to cover events that shape our Clear Lake community. Tell us what you’d like to see by emailing us or using the Contact Us page.

Please be patient as we roll out updates—this platform will evolve with your feedback. Reach out anytime at thesignal@uhcl.edu.
