UHCL Showcases Graduating Artists with Campus Exhibit & Grants

By Joshua Lister

March 31, 2025

The University of Houston–Clear Lake is honoring its senior art majors with a gallery-style exhibition at the Bayou Theater, showcasing capstone projects across painting, sculpture, and digital media.

Alongside the exhibit, graduating students are competing for professional development grants funded by faculty mentors and community sponsors to support their transition into creative careers.

This event underscores UHCL’s commitment to the arts and provides emerging artists with both exposure and essential resources as they prepare to enter the professional art world.
